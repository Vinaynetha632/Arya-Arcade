About Us

Arya Arcade features a modern shopping mall with vanilla stores, anchor stores, a food court, a gaming zone, and a two-screen cineplex. Complementing the retail experience is a 52-room business-class hotel, banquet spaces, and a dedicated fleet of ninety-nine 1BHK and 2BHK service apartments, catering to professionals, medical visitors, and business travellers. Considering the close proximity of the project to 5 major hospitals, several large educational institutions and many government projects, these inventories will cater to the large requirement of temporary accommodation arising in the area. The project also includes an exclusive residential block, providing comfortable living with easy access to daily conveniences and top-tier institutions.

With North Guwahati rapidly transforming into a major economic and institutional hub, Arya Arcade stands at the epicentre of this growth, offering unmatched connectivity, high investment potential, and an all-encompassing lifestyle. Whether for business, residence, or investment, Arya Arcade is where convenience meets opportunity, and modern living meets limitless possibilities.
